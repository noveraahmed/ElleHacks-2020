YOUR RIDE TO SUSTAINABILITY

Our mandate is to provide an extension to the current public transporation system as a means of incentivizing it's usage. Our goal is to re-imagine using PRESTO as a way riding the transit sustainably rather than simply a mean of fare.

WHAT IS TRANSIT+

Say Hello to the new, modern way of travelling fees around the Greater Toronto Area, Hamilton, and Ottawa. Transit+ is a collaborative project with PRESTO to provide you with the advantages you deserve. For every tap or ride, there's some sort of cumulative reward for your action as a participating member initiating in an environmentally-approved act.

By using the presto as a means to reward riders for using transit, on a more consistent basis, it also helps towards reducing carbon emissions and our footprint by increased ridership through incentivizing the citizens to use the ttc more often that they use other unsustainable means of transport.
